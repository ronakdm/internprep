UW STAT Internship Preparation Program

The Internship Preparation Program aims to help students in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington find internships in industry. The first program was organized by [Lang Liu](liu16@uw.edu) and [Ronak Mehta](ronakdm@uw.edu) in Autumn 2022. We held a workshop to explain the overall process of internship applications and provide :doc:`resources` and tips for interviews. We also conducted mock interviews and connect participants with stats alumni current working in industry.
