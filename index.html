Internship Preparation Program
Get Involved

The Internship Preparation Program aims to help students in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington find internships in industry. The first program was organized by Lang Liu and Ronak Mehta in Autumn 2022. We held a workshop to explain the overall process of internship applications and provide Resources and tips for interviews. We also conduct mock interviews and connect participants with stats alumni current working in industry.
